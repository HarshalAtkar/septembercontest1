Ayodhya is a city situated on the banks of the Sarayu river in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. It is the birthplace of Lord Rama and is considered one of the seven sacred cities of the Hindus 1. The city was historically known as Saketa and was an economic hub in ancient times, located at the junction of important caravan routes 1. Lord Rama, also known as Maryada Purushottam Prabhu Shri Ram, is considered the Dharma Praneta of all Yugas. He is regarded as a hero who set out examples for mankind to follow 2. He is one of the most popular avatars of Vishnu and is considered the Supreme Being in Rama-centric traditions of Hinduism

  • Kanak Bhawan:

    • This temple is dedicated to Lord Rama and his wife, Goddess Sita
